Volara Log

A professional flight logbook for pilots

What it does

Volara Log is a personal flight logbook app for licensed and student pilots. It helps you record flight, duty, and simulator time, track regulatory limits and currencies, and export your logbook in EASA, GCAA, FAA/Jeppesen, or UK CAA format. Your data stays on your device, with an optional sync to your own Google Drive so you can back it up or use the app across multiple devices.
